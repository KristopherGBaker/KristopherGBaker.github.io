Expanding subscription value with long-distance delivery

iOS DRI for Long-Distance Delivery — extending Wolt+ value to users outside the free delivery radius with discounted pricing, kept consistent across discovery, venue, and checkout. Led API and product design discussions across teams.

Context

Wolt+ offered free delivery within a defined radius, but users outside that range saw limited benefit — reducing the product's appeal in certain markets.

The problem

We needed to extend the value of Wolt+ to users just outside the free delivery range by introducing discounted delivery — while keeping the experience consistent across discovery, venue pages, and checkout.

Approach

As client DRI, I led the iOS implementation and contributed to the overall API and product design.

Key areas I focused on:

  • aligning frontend and backend on how delivery pricing and eligibility should be represented across surfaces
  • working with the Discovery team to ensure consistent behavior across all venue lists
  • designing a reusable bottom sheet for explaining delivery pricing and savings

I also led discussions around API structure and naming, coordinating across teams to reach a solution that worked for both backend and client constraints.

Outcome

  • Strong positive impact in A/B tests
  • Increased perceived value of Wolt+ in markets outside the core delivery radius
  • Feature successfully rolled out across multiple surfaces in the app

My role

Client DRI, leading implementation, RFC contributions, and cross-team coordination.
